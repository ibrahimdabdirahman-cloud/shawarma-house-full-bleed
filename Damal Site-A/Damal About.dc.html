Why Damal?

At Damal, we pride ourselves on bringing the authentic flavours of Somalia to your table. Established with the passion to share the rich culinary traditions of our homeland, Damal offers an array of dishes that not only tantalize your taste buds but also tell a story of heritage and culture.

Our menu is a testament to the diverse and flavorful nature of Somali cuisine. One of our signature dishes, Haniid, is a must-try. This succulent, slow-cooked lamb is marinated with a blend of traditional spices, delivering an unforgettable taste experience.

"Bringing Flavour And Spice From The Cities Of Mogadishu and Hargeisa To The Vibrant Streets of East London."
