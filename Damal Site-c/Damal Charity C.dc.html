Our Mission

Food as a Force for Good

Damal was founded not just as a restaurant, but as a community hub. We are committed to supporting our local East London community through charitable initiatives — from feeding the vulnerable to supporting Somali youth organisations.

With every order you place, a portion goes towards our community fund.
