Our Story

"A taste of home, served with pride in East London"

At Damal, we pride ourselves on bringing the authentic flavours of Somalia to your table. Established with the passion to share the rich culinary traditions of our homeland, every dish tells a story of heritage and culture.

Our signature dish, Haniid, is a must-try. This succulent, slow-cooked lamb is marinated with a blend of traditional spices, delivering an unforgettable taste experience.
